## Phase 1: Learn

The Researcher subagent searches documentation, repos, and web sources
to produce a structured technical briefing you can use before a partner call.

### What happens

1. You describe the topic or question
2. The Researcher searches multiple sources in parallel
3. You receive a structured briefing with SDK versions, limitations, and recommendations

### Time: ~2 minutes

Click **Research a Topic** below to open Copilot Chat with a sample
research prompt.
