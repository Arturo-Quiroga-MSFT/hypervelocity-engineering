## Phase 2: Demo

The Streamlit Dashboard generator creates a runnable demo app from
a plain English description of what you want to show a partner.

### What happens

1. You describe the demo scenario and data flow
2. The agent generates a complete Streamlit app with mock data
3. You run it locally and present to the partner in real time

### Time: ~5 minutes

Click **Build a Dashboard** below to open Copilot Chat with a sample
demo description.
