## Phase 1: Visualize

The Architecture Diagram builder turns plain English descriptions into
professional Mermaid diagrams you can embed in docs or presentations.

### What happens

1. You describe the architecture in natural language
2. The agent generates a Mermaid diagram with proper Azure service icons and data flows
3. You get a shareable diagram ready for partner meetings
